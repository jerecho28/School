What is cebu eastern college?

Cebu Eastern College (CEC) is a private,
co-educational Chinese-Filipino school in Cebu City.
Its roots can be traced to the Cebu Chinese School,
founded in 1915, and the Cebu Sun
Yat Sen High School, founded in 1925.

IT MISSION

IT Department is committed to honing the capability of the youth
to become useful participants and leaders in the field of work,
entrepreneurial, and technical expertise.

IT VISION

The Information Technology (IT) Department shall be the Center
of Development in IT Education, producing proficient graduates
committed to serve the nation and the world.

IT GOAL

To produce physically, mentally, and morally prepared
IT professionals trained through an environment conducive to
learning with well-trained faculty, appropriate facilities, and affordable tuition fee.
